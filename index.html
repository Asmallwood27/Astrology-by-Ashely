The Zodiac

The zodiac is composed of 12 signs. These signs are all based on constellations and you are most likely familiar with your sun sign. All 12 signs are listed as either masculine or feminine and are considered fixed, cardinal or mutable. Each sign is also representative of the four elements : fire, air, water and earth.Each sign has what is considered a planetary ruler as well. Keep this information in mind while learning more about the signs as it will help you to better understand them.You can click the below for more information on your unique sign.
